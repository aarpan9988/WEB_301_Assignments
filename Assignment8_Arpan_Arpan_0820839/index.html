Introduction

I visited J&K two years ago with my family and friends. I t was best decision I had at that time. Jammu and Kashmir is like a fairy tale with huge mountains covered in snow and stunning valleys.

Favourites

What I liked the most over there were the stunning landscapes, friendly people, and the delicious Kashmiri food. The mountains and valleys were like a dream, the locals were welcoming, and trying dishes like Rogan Josh was a highlight of the trip

Amazing

We also went to some holy places like Vaishno Devi and Shankaracharya Temple. The trek to Vaishno Devi was tough but totally worth it. It felt amazing to reach the sacred cave at the end.
