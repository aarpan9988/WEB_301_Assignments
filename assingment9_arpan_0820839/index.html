PATHAN

Indian RAW agent "Pathaan" (Shah Rukh Khan) gets to know of a major impending attack against India, mounted by a mercenary group led by the ruthless enigma Jim (John Abraham), who has a history of his own. With the doomsday clock ticking away and an agent Rubai (Deepika Padukone) his only possible ally, Pathaan must fight countless betrayals and deal with destruction as he takes on Jim.

Raees

Raees (Shah Rukh Khan), from Fatehpur, Gujarat gets involved in illegal liquor trade at a very young age. Along with Sadiq (Mohammed Zeeshan Ayyub), Raees works for a notorious gangster Jairaj (Atul Kulkarni), smuggles alcohol illegally by bribing the police. Though he is a smuggler, he lives by the philosophy, as taught by his mother (Sheeba Chaddha) that every occupation is good, and no religion is greater than any occupation till it does not cause any harm to anyone.
